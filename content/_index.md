My passion for music lies in the delicacy of its craft. We know that singing brings people together, but for the individual it is also incredibly powerful. It pushes us to harness our own instrument, granting us incredible freedom, steadfastness, strength, and an ability to narrate our own story.

This principle, paired with efficient Estill informed practice, is the spirit of why I choose to teach, and why I chose to start building up other singers, just as I was built up by my own voice teachers. Singing fulfills me, but teaching inspires me. If you are reading this, I hope you are ready to begin your singing journey!

---

**My Mission:** My highest goal is to provide stellar, physiologically informed vocal practice; I aim to instill confidence, integrity, and stamina, both in singing and in all areas of life.
