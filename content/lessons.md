## Age Requirements

Being a one-on-one focused environment, I recommend my studio for those who are ready and excited to learn! Teaching methods and materials are catered to different ages and skill levels.

If you are unsure if your child is ready for one-on-one lessons, I suggest scheduling a trial lesson to test it out. My general recommendation based on my experience is that one-on-one lessons most benefit those who are **8 and older**.
